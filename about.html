About Us

Our Story:

The Birth of a School

Shalom International School was founded in the year 1997 by Sir (Hon.) Chidi Amadi and Dr. Lady Vidal Amadi, who shared a passion for giving young learners a strong moral and academic foundation. Starting with just 18 pupils, 4 dedicated teachers, and 4 classrooms, the school was created to provide a nurturing environment where children could grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

What began as a simple dream quickly became a mission: to raise a new generation of confident, disciplined, and academically sound children.

The Formative Years

In the early years, Shalom International School focused heavily on strong teaching standards and personalized attention. With a small but committed staff, the school cultivated a learning culture centred on:

  • Academic excellence
  • Character and moral development
  • Clean, safe, and supportive classrooms
  • Strong relationships between teachers, pupils, and parents

These formative years shaped the identity of the school and laid the groundwork for future growth.

Becoming Strong and Stable

As the school's reputation grew, so did its population. The addition of new classrooms, improved learning materials, and more qualified staff helped the school transition from a small community institution to one of the most respected schools in Port Harcourt.

Key milestones during this phase included:

  • Expansion from a handful of learners to hundreds of pupils
  • Recruitment of trained, passionate educators
  • Introduction of structured academic programmes aligned with national standards
  • Improved administrative and support systems

This period marked Shalom as a reliable, stable, and well-organized place of learning.

Continuing to Grow and Thrive

Today, Shalom International School has become a thriving educational community with modern facilities, including:

  • A fully equipped ICT centre
  • A science laboratory
  • Fine arts and music studios
  • A home economics laboratory
  • A well-stocked library

With over 500 pupils, dozens of classrooms, and a strong team of teaching and non-teaching staff, the school continues to maintain excellence in academic performance, extracurricular activities, and character-building programmes.

Our curriculum blends the best of Nigerian educational standards with select global practices, ensuring learners receive a balanced, world-class education.

Our Commitment to the Future

As we advance, Shalom International School remains committed to:

  • Providing quality, affordable education
  • Embracing technology and modern teaching methods
  • Building confident learners ready for secondary school and beyond
  • Strengthening our culture of discipline, integrity, and excellence
  • Expanding opportunities for creativity, leadership, and skills development

Our focus is not just to educate children—but to prepare them for life.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence-in academics, character, and service
  • Discipline-shaping responsible and respectful learners
  • Integrity-doing what is right at all times
  • Creativity-encouraging pupils to imagine, explore, and innovate
  • Community-fostering teamwork between pupils, staff, and families

A Place Where Every Child Matters

At Shalom International School, every child is valued, celebrated, and supported. We believe that every learner carries potential, and our role is to help them discover and nurture it.

We are more than a school — we are a family, a community, and a beacon of growth.
