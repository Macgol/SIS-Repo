Academics

Academics

At Shalom International School, our academic programme is built to shape confident, well-rounded learners through a blend of rigorous academics, creativity, and practical skill development. Each level of schooling is tailored to the age, needs, and abilities of our learners, ensuring that every child receives the support and guidance they need to excel.

Early Years / Pre-School

Our Early Years Foundation Stage focuses on nurturing curiosity, imagination, and social skills. Through play-based learning, structured routines, and hands-on activities, children build essential pre-academic skills that prepare them for primary education.

Key Focus Areas

  • Literacy and phonics readiness
  • Numeracy foundations
  • Social and emotional development
  • Creative expression through art, music, and movement
  • Sensorial and motor skill development

Primary School

Our Primary School programme develops core academic skills while encouraging independence and problem-solving. Lessons are interactive, technology-supported, and designed to build strong foundations in all key subject areas.

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science & Technology
  • National Values Education
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical & Health Education

Highlights

  • Small class sizes
  • Activity-based learning
  • Weekly assessments and feedback

Secondary School

Our Secondary School offers a solid academic pathway aligned with WAEC/NECO standards while also preparing students for future careers and higher education. Students are exposed to critical thinking, advanced subject areas, and practical experiences that help them develop into responsible young adults.

Junior Secondary

  • English Studies
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science
  • Business Studies
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Studies
  • Pre-Vocational Subjects (Catering, Graphics, Tailoring, Bead-Making, Leather Craft, Cosmetology)

Senior Secondary (Science, Arts & Commercial Options)

  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Further Maths, ICT
  • Arts: Literature, Government, CRS, History, Fine Art
  • Commercial:Financial Accounting, Commerce, Economics, Marketing

Highlights

  • Career Guidance & Mentorship
  • Practical Skills Training
  • Leadership Development Programmes
  • Clubs & Co-Curricular Activities

Special Students Section (Autistic & Deaf Students)

Shalom International School is an inclusive environment committed to giving every child an opportunity to succeed. Our Special Students Programme provides individualized learning support for children with autism and children who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Autistic Learners

We create structured, calm, and supportive learning environments tailored to each child's needs.

Support Features

  • Individual Education Plans (IEPs)
  • Behaviour and social-skills coaching
  • Sensory-friendly classrooms
  • One-on-one instruction where necessary
  • Visual learning tools

Deaf / Hard of Hearing Learners

We ensure full access to education through communication support and specialized instruction.

Support Features

  • Sign-language trained teachers
  • Visual teaching aids and interactive tools
  • Assistive learning devices (where applicable)
  • Inclusive classroom engagement

Our Goal
To build confidence, independence, and academic growth for all learners—regardless of learning differences.
