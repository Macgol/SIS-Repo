Curriculum

At Shalom International School, we offer a rich and well-structured curriculum that supports academic excellence, creativity, and character development. Our teaching follows national standards while integrating modern learning methods to prepare students for the future.

Pre-School / Early Years

Our early years learning focuses on exploration, play-based learning, and early literacy.

  • Literacy & Numeracy Foundations
  • Phonics & Reading
  • Social and Emotional Development
  • Creative Arts
  • Outdoor Activities & Motor Skills

Primary School

A balanced curriculum that builds confidence and broad knowledge.

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science & Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Civic Education
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical & Health Education
  • Cultural & Religious Studies
  • Computer Studies / ICT
