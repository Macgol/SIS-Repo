Admissions & Enrollment

Welcome to Shalom International School

We are delighted that you are considering Shalom International School for your child. Our admission process is simple, transparent, and designed to help families make the best educational choice. Whether you are enrolling a Nursery learner, a Primary pupil,or a High School student, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Why Enrol With Us?

At Shalom International School, we offer:

  • A safe and nurturing learning environment
  • Strong academic curriculum with modern teaching methods
  • Qualified and caring teachers
  • Clean, well-equipped classrooms
  • ICT, Science, Music, Library and Creative Art facilities
  • Balanced extracurricular and character-building activities
  • Affordable fees with flexible payment plans

Give your child the foundation they deserve.

Who Can Apply?

We welcome learners into the following classes:

  • Nursery (1-3)
  • Basic / Primary (1-6)
  • High School / Secondary (Jss1-Ss3)
  • Special Classes (Autistic & Deaf)

Placement is based on age, readiness, and assessment results.

Admission Requirements

Parents/Guardians are required to provide:

  • Completed admission form
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Last school's result (for transfer pupils)
  • Medical fitness report

All prospective learners may undergo a short placement test to determine the appropriate class.

How to Enrol

Our enrollment process is simple:

  1. Visit the School Office
    2. Pick up the admission form or make inquiries with our admin staff.
  2. Fill and Submit the Form
    3. Submit completed forms with required documents.
  3. Assessment / Interview
    4. Your child may take a brief assessment to determine suitability.
  4. Payment of Acceptance Fee
    5. Once accepted, proceed with the acceptance/registration fee.
  5. Receive Starter Pack & Resumption Details
    6. You will get a welcome pack containing uniforms, book lists, and term information.

School Fees

Our fees are affordable and structured to accommodate families within the community.
For a detailed breakdown of termly fees, please visit the school office or contact our admin line.
