School Policies

Guiding principles that ensure a safe, disciplined, and supportive learning environment.

Overview of Our School Policies

Our policies are designed to maintain order, protect student welfare, and uphold the high standards of Shalom International School. These guidelines apply to all students, staff, parents, and visitors.

Attendance & Punctuality
  • Students must attend school regularly and arrive on time.
  • Parents should inform the school of absences in advance.
  • Medical reports are required for long absences.
  • Repeated lateness may lead to disciplinary action.
School Uniform & Appearance
  • Proper school uniform must be worn daily.
  • Extreme hairstyles or distracting accessories are not allowed.
  • Students must maintain personal cleanliness.
Classroom Behaviour
  • Respect for teachers and classmates is mandatory.
  • Students must submit assignments on time.
  • No noise-making, bullying, or fighting.
Code of Conduct
  • Bullying or harassment is strictly prohibited.
  • Stealing or vandalism is not tolerated.
  • No dangerous or prohibited items allowed.
Use of School Facilities
  • School facilities must be handled responsibly.
  • No eating in laboratories or restricted areas.
  • Damage must be reported immediately.
ICT & Device Policy
  • Computers are for academic use only.
  • No cyberbullying or accessing inappropriate content.
  • Personal phones are restricted during school hours.
Health & Safety
  • The Sick Bay is available for minor health issues.
  • Parents will be contacted for serious cases.
  • Allergies must be reported to the school.
Parent–School Partnership
  • Parents should attend school meetings regularly.
  • Maintain open communication with teachers.
  • Pickup and drop-off times must be respected.
Consequences for Misconduct
  • Warnings (verbal or written)
  • Loss of privileges
  • Suspension or expulsion for severe cases
Policy Updates

Policies may be reviewed and updated when necessary. Parents and guardians will be notified of any changes.
