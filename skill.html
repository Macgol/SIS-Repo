Skill Acquisition Programme

Building creativity, confidence, and entrepreneurial passion in our learners.

At Shalom International School, our Skill Acquisition Programme equips students with hands-on abilities that prepare them for real-world creativity and productivity.

The programme is optional for Primary (4–6) pupils and compulsory for Secondary students, who select one skill per academic year.

Leather Craft

Students learn to create belts, purses, key holders, and simple footwear.

Cosmetology

Students receive training in skincare, makeup basics, nail care, and hygiene practices.

Dressmaking / Tailoring

Students learn sewing, pattern making, fabric selection, and basic clothing production.

Graphics Design

Students gain digital art skills, creating posters, flyers, logos, and brand designs.

Bead Making

Students design beautiful necklaces, bracelets, and decorative accessories.

Catering / Baking

Students learn food preparation, kitchen hygiene, pastry making, and presentation skills.

Benefits of the Programme

  • Improves creativity and self-expression
  • Teaches real-life work skills
  • Boosts entrepreneurship interest
  • Builds teamwork and discipline
  • Develops confidence and originality

Programme Structure

Primary (Year 4–6): Optional, fun introductory sessions.

Secondary: Compulsory. Students choose one skill per academic year.
